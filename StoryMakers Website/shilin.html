Shilin Story Chronicle
士林故事誌

Transforming Shilin's Stories into Cultural Energy

About the Project

What is Shilin Story Chronicle?

Shilin Story Chronicle (士林故事誌) is our flagship project dedicated to preserving and revitalizing the rich cultural heritage of Shilin District in Taipei. Shilin is a district built on stories—from the bustling markets of the Qing Dynasty, to the multi-religious gathering spaces around Cixian Temple, to the modernization traces of official residences, to the world-famous night market, and the living traces of old streets and markets.

However, under the pressure of rapid urban renewal and commercial transformation, these stories remain scattered in residents' memories, not yet forming systematic narratives, and not successfully transformed into industrial value.

項目概述

士林故事誌是我們致力於保存與振興台北士林區豐富文化遺產的旗艦專案。士林是一座由故事建構而成的城市—從清代大市集的繁盛、慈諴宮與多宗教匯聚的信仰場域、官邸的現代化軌跡、夜市的世界知名度，到老街與市場的生活痕跡。

然而在快速的都市更新與商業轉型壓力下，這些故事多半仍散落在居民記憶中，未形成系統化的敘事，更未成功轉化為產業價值。

Project Components

Story Collection | 故事採集

We systematically collect oral histories, community memories, and cultural narratives from Shilin residents. Through workshops, interviews, and community events, we document the stories that define Shilin's unique identity.

我們系統性地收集士林居民的口述歷史、社區記憶與文化敘事。透過工作坊、訪談與社區活動，我們記錄定義士林獨特身份的故事。

Writing Hub | 寫作平台

Our Story Writing Hub serves as a creative space where community members can develop their narrative skills, participate in writing workshops, and contribute to the collective storytelling of Shilin. This platform facilitates cross-generational exchange and oral history documentation.

我們的寫作平台作為創意空間，讓社區成員發展敘事能力、參與寫作工作坊，並為士林的集體敘事做出貢獻。這個平台促進跨世代交流與口述史記錄。

Local Living Store | 生活商店

The Local Living Store combines stories, space, industry, and community. It's a physical space where Shilin's narratives are transformed into products, experiences, and cultural offerings that support local economy while preserving cultural heritage.

生活商店結合故事、空間、產業與社區。這是一個實體空間，將士林的敘事轉化為支持在地經濟同時保存文化遺產的產品、體驗與文化服務。

Cultural Mapping / AR | 文化地圖 / AR

Through innovative technology partnerships with WPI and Soochow University, we create interactive cultural maps and AR experiences that bring Shilin's stories to life. Visitors can explore the district's history through immersive digital experiences.

透過與WPI及東吳大學的創新科技合作，我們創造互動式文化地圖與AR體驗，讓士林的故事活起來。訪客可以透過沉浸式數位體驗探索這個區域的歷史。

Community Programs | 社區計畫

We organize various community programs including storytelling workshops, cultural tours, artisan collaborations, and youth engagement initiatives. These programs strengthen community bonds and empower residents to become active narrators of their own stories.

我們組織各種社區計畫，包括敘事工作坊、文化導覽、職人合作與青年參與計畫。這些計畫強化社區連結，賦權居民成為自己故事的積極敘述者。

International Collaboration

Partnership with WPI & Soochow University

Through collaboration with Worcester Polytechnic Institute (WPI) and Soochow University, we integrate systematic field research, 3D/XR/AR technologies, and multicultural observations. This partnership forms four major collaboration modules that promote the internationalization of Shilin's stories.

  • Systematic Field Research
  • 3D/XR/AR Technology Integration
  • Multicultural Observation & Analysis
  • International Storytelling Platforms

與WPI及東吳大學的合作

透過與WPI（伍斯特理工學院）及東吳大學的合作，我們整合系統化田野調查、3D/XR/AR技術與多文化觀察。這個合作形成四大合作模組，推動士林故事的國際化。

  • 系統化田野調查
  • 3D/XR/AR技術整合
  • 多文化觀察與分析
  • 國際敘事平台
