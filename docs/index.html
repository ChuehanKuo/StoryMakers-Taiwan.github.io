Our Mission

Transforming Stories into Cultural Energy

At StoryMakers Taiwan, we believe that every community holds unique stories that shape its identity and future. Our mission is to collect, preserve, and transform these local narratives into powerful cultural assets that drive social change and economic growth.

Through our innovative approach combining storytelling, design, technology, and community engagement, we create sustainable models for urban regeneration and cultural preservation.

我們的使命

故事造城相信，每個社區都擁有獨特的故事，這些故事塑造了社區的身份認同與未來。我們的使命是收集、保存並轉化這些在地敘事，成為推動社會改變與經濟成長的文化資產。

透過結合敘事、設計、科技與社區參與的創新方法，我們為都市再生與文化保存創造可持續的模式。

What We Do
