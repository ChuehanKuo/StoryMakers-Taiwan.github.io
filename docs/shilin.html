Food Guide

Taste Shilin through its stories. Each dish carries a piece of local history and community memory.

Shilin Sausage

A night market icon since the 1950s. This grilled Taiwanese sausage tells the story of post-war Taiwan's street food culture and the vendors who shaped Shilin's night market identity.

Fried Chicken Steak

The oversized fried chicken steak that made Shilin famous. Born from street vendor innovation, this dish represents Taiwan's creative food culture and the entrepreneurial spirit of Shilin's night market community.

Oyster Omelet

A Fujianese heritage dish that became a Taiwanese classic. In Shilin, this dish connects generations and represents the district's role as a gathering place for diverse communities.

Bubble Tea

While bubble tea originated elsewhere, Shilin's night market became one of its early adoption centers. The story of bubble tea in Shilin reflects Taiwan's drink innovation culture.

Stinky Tofu

A bold flavor that divides opinions, but beloved by locals. The story of stinky tofu vendors in Shilin represents resilience, tradition, and the unapologetic authenticity of local street food culture.

Traditional Ice Cream Roll

A nostalgic treat that brings back childhood memories. This simple dessert tells stories of Shilin's markets as community gathering spaces where families shopped, socialized, and created memories.
